About Ohio Teleprompter

Our Story

Ohio Teleprompter brings professional teleprompting services to the Buckeye State as part of the National Teleprompter network. With over 25 years of experience serving clients across the country, we deliver the same level of excellence that has made us trusted partners for Fortune 500 companies, sports organizations, broadcast networks, and production companies nationwide.

Our expansion into Ohio represents our commitment to providing local expertise backed by national resources. Whether you're producing a corporate event in Columbus, a broadcast in Cleveland, or a conference in Cincinnati, you get experienced operators, current-generation equipment, and seamless execution.
